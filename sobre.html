Why we built LusoPanda

A clear goal: help students integrate faster.

Main goal:

Make Portuguese learning easier and more inclusive for students who start from zero.

  • Support students with clear English help (no confusion).
  • Teach real phrases used at school, cafeteria, and with friends.
  • Use short games to motivate and give feedback.
  • Keep the website accessible and easy to navigate.
